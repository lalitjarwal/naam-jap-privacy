Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Naam Jap ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Voice Recordings: Audio files are recorded and stored locally on your device. We do not upload these recordings to any server.
  • App Settings: Preferences like goals and themes are stored locally using shared_preferences.

B. Information Collected Automatically

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Collects anonymous usage data to help us improve app features.
  • Google AdMob: Uses device identifiers to serve personalized advertisements.

2. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone: For mantra recordings.
  • Notifications: For chanting reminders.
  • Storage: To save and play recordings locally.

3. Data Storage and Security

All personal data and recordings remain on your device. We do not maintain any servers to store your chanting data.
